OVERVIEW
VIT is a cloud-based, IoT enterprise risk management platform that reduces the burden of paperwork and use of lagging indicators that the safety industry has relied on for the last 50 years. Our mission is to take safety out of the dark ages and empower organizations with leading indicators and utilize modern data processing techniques to solve complex and difficult challenges that plague the workplace.
