WHAT WE DO

We provide free, online, interactive computer science curriculum for high schools that is engaging, creative, rigorous and fun. CS Academy also includes teacher training, an online interactive textbook, online technical support and more!

FEATURES

For Students For Teachers

Interactive Notes

Our interactive notes include interactive questions and coding exercises that give students the power to test their own understanding and pace themselves.

In-Browser & Text-based Python Programming

Our platform integrates Brython, a powerful in-browser python interpreter, to allow students to write real Python code from any browser

Image Inspector Tool

CS Academy's Image Inspector Tool empowers students to understand what their code is doing in an interactive and visual way.

CMU student-authored graphical content

Students in CS Academy learn by working on fun, graphics and animations problems developed by a team of CMU students.

Online Support for teachers by CMU

We have an online community where teachers can collaborate and get questions answered by a team of CMU Students.

Autograded Exercises

All of our excercises are autograded, allowing teachers to spend less time grading work and more time helping students.

Teacher Portal + Gradebook

Our tools give teachers the ability to manage every student's individual progress and observe how the class is doing at a glance.

(PLN) Network of CS Academy Teachers

We connect CS Academy teachers allowing them to collaborate, share, and learn.
